Documentary Feature / Uttarakhand

Fading Footsteps

An eight-month observational documentary capturing the culture, traditions, and daily lives of the Palsi shepherds in the Western Himalayas. The project showcases long-term immersive filmmaking in one of India's most challenging environments.

  • Winner: Asian Talent Int. Film Festival, 6th Reels International Film Festival
  • Official Selection: India, UK, Spain, Nepal, Denmark, France
Documentary Short / Tamil Nadu

Under The Scorching Sun

Short documentary based on life of people who work in the third largest salt production in Tamilnadu, Marakkanam salt pans. Prepared as part of Masters project work showcasing corrosive, scorching evaporation ponds yielding crystal salt.
